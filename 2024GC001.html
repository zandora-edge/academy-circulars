Zan Academy General Circular: 2024/GC/001
Category: Academic Announcement
Dated December 2, 2024
Academic Affairs Office Kerala, IN

Circular

Dear Students,

It is hereby notified for the information of all concerned that the results of the following examinations have been published:

  • Final Examination for the Batch 2021–2024 (Artificial Intelligence).
  • Mid-Term Examination for the Batch 2022–2025 (AIDS).

The results are now available on the official website: results.zan.academy,
Diploma certificates for the batch 2021-2024 and transcript for both batches will be issued in due course.

If you have any query, please contact the Academic Affairs Office at professor@zandora.org.

Important Instructions:

  • Students wishing to apply for revaluation or digital copy of their answer sheets must write an email to the Academic Affairs Office with:
    • A copy of the payment receipt issued by the Affiliated Center where they are registered.
    • Complete details of their application (name, registration number, course, subject/paper code).
  • Applications must be submitted on or before December 12, 2024. Late applications will not be considered under any circumstances.
  • Students whose results are marked as "Failed," "Withheld," or those who did not appear for the examination shall await further communication from the academy. A separate circular will be issued regarding retests, providing additional opportunities for these students.

Fee Structure:

  • ₹125/- for application fee
  • ₹735/- for revaluation fee
  • ₹225/- for digital copy fee (digital copies will be available only after the whole revaluation is completed)
Note : Defective applications (shortage of payment slip/ incomplete in details) shall summarily be rejected without notice.

Dr. Mohammed Musthafa (h.c)
Principal & Head of the Institution (HoI)
